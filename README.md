# Legal Documents

This directory is for Bento's public legal documents.

The goal is to keep our terms, policies, and related legal pages easy for anyone to read, while also making changes transparent through the repository's commit history.

## Why this exists

- Keep public legal terms in the codebase
- Make legal changes easy to review in pull requests
- Preserve a clear version history for every update

## Included documents

- Core terms and subscription terms
- Privacy, cookies, and acceptable use policies
- API, copyright, abuse reporting, and SMS compliance policies
- Security overview, government requests policy, and subprocessor list
