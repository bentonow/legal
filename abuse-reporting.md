---

If you believe someone is using Bento to send spam, phishing, abusive messages, infringing content, or other harmful material, please report it to us at `report@bentonow.com`.

## What to include

To help us investigate, please include as much of the following as you can:

- the email, SMS, campaign, account, domain, or URL you are reporting;
- the date and time you received or observed the activity;
- copies of the message, including full headers, screenshots, or other evidence where available;
- a short explanation of why you believe the activity is abusive, unlawful, deceptive, or otherwise violates our policies; and
- your contact details if you are willing to answer follow-up questions.

## What we may do

We may review the report, request additional information, investigate the account or content, and take action under our Terms, Acceptable Use Policy, SMS Messaging Compliance Policy, or Copyright Policy.

We may not be able to share the outcome of every investigation, but we do review abuse reports and may act where appropriate.

## Other requests

- For privacy requests, contact `support@bentonow.com`.
- For copyright or intellectual property complaints, refer to our Copyright Policy.
- For legal process or government requests, refer to our Government Requests Policy.
